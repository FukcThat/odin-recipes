Grilled Cheese

Description

Rice, broccoli, and chimken is a well-balanced meal composed of fluffy rice, steamed broccoli, and tender chimken (chicken) pieces. This dish offers a harmonious blend of flavors and textures, making it a satisfying and nutritious option for a meal.

Ingredients

  • White Instant Rice
  • Broccoli
  • Chimken Chunks
  • Sesame Oil
  • Greated Garlic
  • Grated Ginger
  • Grated or finely chopped Chilli
  • Soy Sauce
  • Maggi No 3 or any Chicken Seasoning

    • Steps

    1. Microwave the Rice for 2 Minutes on 800W or as directed on the packaging.
    2. Heat sesame oil in a pan and fry garlic, ginger and chilli until fragrant.
    3. Add Chimken Chunks and fry until golden brown.
    4. Cut broccoli florrettes off stalk, peel stalk and roughly chop, then add all to pan and fry until lightly charred.
    5. Add soy sauce and chicken seasoning to taste.
    6. Plate rice in a bowl, top with chicken and broccoli.
    7. Optional: Garnish with Sesame seeds.
