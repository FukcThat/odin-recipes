Shepherd's Pie

Description

Shepherd's pie is a classic British dish consisting of ground or minced meat (typically lamb or beef) mixed with vegetables and a rich gravy, topped with a layer of creamy mashed potatoes. It's then baked until the top is golden brown, creating a hearty and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Ground Beef
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Frozen Peas
  • Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Paprika Powder
  • Red Wine
  • Potato Starch
  • Tomato Concentrate

Steps

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into pieces. Boil until soft in salted water, then drain.
  2. While the potatoes boil, fry the ground beef until browned in a little bit of oil.
  3. In the meantime, peel and dice Onions and Carrots and add to ground beef. Fry until lightly caramelized.
  4. Add frozen peas and fry until hot again.
  5. Add salt, pepper, tomato concentrate and paprika powder and stir well.
  6. Turn heat on high and add red wine. Let the alcohol cook off for a few seconds.
  7. Siv the potato starch over the pan and stir well.
  8. Mash previously drained Potatoes with a dash of paprika powder and pepper.
  9. Pour Pie Filling from Pan into an oven safe dish and level as well as possible.
  10. Add mashed potatoes on top and smooth out.
  11. Optional: Add texture to mashed potatoes with a fork for crispy edges.
