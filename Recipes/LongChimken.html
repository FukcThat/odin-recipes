Long Chimken

Description

A long chimken sandwich is a popular fast-food item, typically made with a seasoned and breaded chimken patty served in a long, soft roll or baguette-style bread. It's often accompanied by various toppings like lettuce, mayonnaise, and pickles, providing a convenient and savory handheld meal option.

Ingredients

  • Hot-Dog Buns
  • Vegan Mayo
  • Vegan Breded Chicken Strips alternative (aka. Chimken)
  • Lettuce
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter insides of a hot-dog bun and toast in a hot pan.
  2. Wash and cut lettuce into thin strips
  3. Once Buns are toasted, take out of the pan, add some more butter and fry the Chimken.
  4. Spread Mayo on both toasted sides of the bun.
  5. Add fried Chimken and top with shredded lettuce and top bun.
  6. Eat before lettuce gets soggy.

Tip:

Alternatively you can let the fried Chimken cool down and assemble later so it can be packed and eaten later.
