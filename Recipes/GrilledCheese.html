Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese is a simple but delicious sandwich made by placing cheese between two slices of bread, which are then toasted or grilled until the bread turns golden brown and the cheese melts, resulting in a warm, gooey, and satisfying comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter both sides of two slices of toast.
  2. Fry both slices until golden brown on one side and flip.
  3. Add cheese on top of one slice.
  4. Add other slice of toast on top so the fried side touches the cheese.
  5. Flip every once in a while until both sides are golden brown and cheese has melted.
  6. Serve hot, maybe garnish with parsley.
